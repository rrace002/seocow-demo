About Red Rabbit Security

Established in 2018 and headquartered in White Plains, NY, Red Rabbit Security delivers enterprise-class cybersecurity, managed IT, cloud, compliance, backup, VoIP, AI, and software services to small and medium-sized businesses.

Who we are

We started Red Rabbit Security because growing businesses need the same protection and reliability as larger enterprises — without the complexity or inflated pricing of traditional enterprise providers. Today we help organizations modernize technology, secure systems, improve resilience, and operate with confidence across the United States, Canada, and Mexico.

Our founder, Marc Weathers, is the author of Phishing, Vishing, and Smishing, Oh My! — a practical guide to social-engineering awareness that reflects how we teach and operate: clear, actionable, and grounded in real attacks.

What we believe

  • Security is layered — not a single product purchase
  • SMBs deserve enterprise-grade outcomes at accessible rates
  • Reporting should be plain English, not fear theater
  • Long-term partnerships beat short-term ticket chasing
